# Privacy Policy for FitPulse **Last Updated: July 6, 2026** FitPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your data privacy and providing you with absolute control over your personal metrics. Unlike traditional fitness applications, FitPulse operates on a **100% offline-first architecture**. All your personal health records, cycle tracking configurations, and metrics are stored strictly in your device's private database sandbox and never transmitted to external servers. --- ### 1. Zero Cloud Data Collection & User Profiles We do not operate remote backend servers, cloud storage clusters, or user account systems. * **No Accounts Required:** You do not need to provide a name, email address, phone number, or login credentials to access 100% of the app's utilities. * **No Silent Tracking:** Your inputs (weights, heights, menstrual dates, cycle lengths, logged workouts) are never uploaded, shared, or compiled outside of your device. --- ### 2. Local Database Storage & Data Risks Your health inputs are persisted locally using an on-device secure **Room SQLite Database**. * **Complete Sandbox Isolation:** Because your metrics reside inside your device's secure application sandbox, they cannot be crawled or read by other external apps. * **Your Backup Responsibility:** Because there is no cloud synchronization or remote backup, deleting the FitPulse application from your device will permanently erase all your historical logs, logs, and period predictions. We cannot recover deleted local logs. --- ### 3. Google AdMob Integration & Strict Policy Compliance To fund ongoing offline development, FitPulse integrates the Google AdMob SDK. AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, advertising IDs, or approximate location details to display compliant advertising. We operate AdMob ads with strict compliance guidelines: * **No Ads on Period Calendars:** To respect your privacy, the Period tracker calendar and prediction dashboard display **zero advertisements**. * **Transition Frequency Capping:** Full-screen interstitial transition ads are controlled by a mandatory **3-minute (180 seconds) cooldown interval** to prevent user disruption and accidental clicks. * **Opt-In Premium Access:** Access to premium elite training programs is initiated solely via user consent by watching an Opt-In Rewarded Video. --- ### 4. Minimal System Permissions FitPulse requests minimal system permissions: * **INTERNET Access:** Used strictly to retrieve AdMob ad inventories and trigger rating/sharing intents directly to the Google Play Store. --- ### 5. Instant Local Data Purging You have the absolute right to delete your personal data at any time. * To erase all logged weights, heights, BMI history, menstrual dates, and workout schedules, navigate to **Settings ➔ "Delete All My Data"** inside the app. This action instantly and permanently wipes out your local SQLite database. --- ### 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will reflect the latest revisions by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. --- ### Contact Developer Support For inquiries, support, or feedback, please contact developer support directly at: * **Email:** support@fitpulse.app
