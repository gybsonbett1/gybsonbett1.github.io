Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

FitPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is dedicated to protecting your data privacy and providing you with absolute control over your personal metrics. Unlike traditional fitness tracking applications, FitPulse operates on a 100% offline-first architecture. This policy details how we preserve data integrity, and the precautions taken to secure your health inputs.

1. Zero Cloud Data Collection

We do not operate remote backend servers, cloud storage clusters, or user profile registration systems.

  • No Profiles Required: You do not need to provide a name, email address, phone number, or credentials to access the app.
  • No Active Logging: Your measurements, cycle dates, and workout histories are processed exclusively on-device.

2. Local Database & Data Storage Rules

All personal metrics—including weights, heights, BMI history, menstrual dates, cycle logs, and training performance logs—remain stored strictly on your device's internal storage sandbox via a private SQLite database managed through Android's secure Room Library.

⚠️ Notice: Because there is no cloud synchronization or remote database backup, deleting the FitPulse application from your device will permanently erase all your historical logs, logs, and menstrual predictions. We cannot recover deleted local logs.

3. Google AdMob Integration & Policy Compliance

To support ongoing independent development, FitPulse integrates the Google AdMob SDK. AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising IDs, or approximate coordinates to display relevant ads. We operate this SDK with strict privacy overrides:

  • No Ads on Menstrual Calendars: To preserve a clean, supportive, and private environment, zero advertisements are displayed inside the Period Tracker calendar dashboard.
  • Transition Frequency Cap: Full-screen interstitial transitions are controlled by a mandatory 3-minute cooldown interval to prevent accidental clicks.
  • Opt-In Access: Premium tier workout programs are unlocked exclusively with user consent via Rewarded Video Ads.

4. App Permissions

FitPulse requests minimal system permissions to operate on your Android device:

  • INTERNET Access: Used strictly to fetch AdMob advertisement inventories and forward optional rating and sharing intents to the Google Play Store.

5. Permanent Local Purging

You have the absolute, immediate right to wipe out your data at any time. To purge all BMI histories, menstrual cycle parameters, and completed workout records, navigate to the application's Settings → "Delete All My Data" menu. This action instantly resets your local SQLite database structure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate revisions by modifying the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
