# Privacy Policy for FitPulse **Effective Date:** July 6, 2026 **Last Updated:** July 6, 2026 At FitPulse ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you download, install, and use our mobile application, **FitPulse** (the "App"), on Android devices. --- ### 1. 100% OFFLINE-FIRST & LOCAL DATA STORAGE FitPulse is designed to be an **offline-first application**. - **No Remote Transmission:** All personal health, menstrual, fitness, and physical metrics—including your Body Mass Index (BMI), height, weight, historical log archives, and menstrual period start/end dates—are stored **100% locally** on your device's private SQLite storage (via Room database persistence). - **No Cloud Database:** We do not operate remote databases, servers, or cloud infrastructure to store or collect your personal data. Your metrics never leave your device. - **Immediate Data Erasure:** You can permanently purge all data locally at any time using the "Delete All My Data" tool in the App's Settings menu. --- ### 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND RECEIVE #### A. Personal Data Because the App operates offline, we do not require user registration, email logins, social account linkages, or name collection. We collect **zero** personally identifiable information (PII). #### B. Permissions Requested To deliver ads and handle standard Android utilities, the App requests: - **`android.permission.INTERNET`**: Required strictly to initialize and load Google AdMob advertisements and redirect users to the Google Play Store for rating or feedback. --- ### 3. THIRD-PARTY UTILITIES & INTEGRATIONS #### Google AdMob We incorporate Google AdMob to deliver visual ads in designated sections (Native and Interstitial layouts) to help support development. - **No Ads in Period Tracker:** To maintain strict contextual privacy, no advertisements of any kind are placed within the Period tracking dashboard. - **Targeting & Ad IDs:** Google AdMob may collect and use advertising identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID or Android ID) according to Google's standard ad policies to provide customized recommendations and frequency capping. - Please review Google’s privacy practices here: [Google Partner Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites). --- ### 4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA COMPLIANCE) FitPulse does not knowingly collect or target any personal data from children under the age of 13. Since all tracking calculations (BMI and period sync) happen completely offline on your own device, no children’s information is transmitted or held by us. --- ### 5.
